Tooltip Wording Variations

The badge is the what. The tooltip is the why. Three tonal approaches.

Wording A - Mission
Buying here supports Product.ai and keeps honest product advice free for everyone. How we make money
Mission-first. Frames the purchase as supporting the cause. "Free for everyone" reinforces the equity angle.
Wording B - Factual
Product.ai earns a commission when you buy through this link. Our recommendations are independent of partnerships. Verify our independence
Straight disclosure. No emotional framing. Links to Verifiable Trust Ledger. Most transparent, least warm.
Wording C - Gratitude
This is a Product.ai partner. Purchasing here helps fund the product you're using right now. Thank you. Our transparency pledge
Direct, warm, grateful. "The product you're using right now" makes the connection concrete. "Thank you" is a bold choice - genuine or needy depending on execution.
Wording D - Hybrid
Product.ai partner. We earn a commission on purchases - this is how we keep everything free. Our recommendations never change based on partnerships. How we make money
Leads with the fact, follows with the mission, closes with the independence claim. Most complete. Longest.
