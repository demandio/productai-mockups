Partner Badge v2: Meaning Explorations

Exploring metaphors that communicate "your purchase has a secondary effect" without communicating "this merchant is better or endorsed." The badge is about the user's agency, not the merchant's status.

Design constraint: The badge must NOT elevate the merchant. It must NOT look like a verification, endorsement, or quality signal. It should communicate a neutral fact (partnership exists) with a positive secondary meaning (your purchase supports something you're already using). Think "fair trade" mark, not "verified seller" badge. The merchant didn't earn the badge by being good. They're simply connected to the ecosystem.
Metaphor 1
Ripple
"Your purchase creates impact beyond the transaction"
A drop in water. Your action radiates outward. No hierarchy, no endorsement - just cause and effect. The purchase is the drop; Product.ai staying free is the ripple. Organic, non-corporate, beautiful at small sizes.

Metaphor 5
Cycle
"Your purchase feeds back into the system you're using"
A circular arrow - not recycling, but a feedback loop. Your money goes to the merchant, a portion flows back to Product.ai, Product.ai stays free for you. It's a closed system. Structural, not emotional. Shows the mechanics without hiding them.
