Ingredient Interactions
Known interactions between ingredients in this formula or with common routine companions.

Niacinamide + L-Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C)
If you use a Vitamin C serum (L-AA form) in the same routine, apply it first and wait 15 minutes. At low pH, niacinamide can convert to niacin, causing flushing. This interaction is often overstated online, but at 10% niacinamide, the risk is real. Alternative: use Vitamin C in the morning, this serum at night.
+
Niacinamide + Zinc PCA -- Synergistic
These two work well together for oily skin. Niacinamide regulates sebum production from the inside; Zinc PCA provides surface-level oil absorption. This is a well-formulated pairing.
