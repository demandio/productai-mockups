Evaluation Notes

Strengths

  • Accessible - transparent, honest, screen-reader ready
  • Same visual weight as existing metadata tags
  • Works at 380px extension width
  • Non-apologetic tone in footer
  • Clear contrast: Partner rows vs non-partner rows

Tradeoffs

  • Adds horizontal content to already-dense rows
  • When most merchants are partners, tags may feel repetitive
  • "Partner" is generic - could mean many things
