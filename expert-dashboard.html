USER QUERY "What's the best magnesium supplement for sleep? I've been taking Nature Made 400mg oxide."
Verdict: Switch from oxide to glycinate form. Your current Nature Made 400mg oxide is delivering approximately 16mg of absorbed magnesium[A1] - about 4% bioavailability. For sleep specifically, magnesium glycinate has dual benefit: the magnesium supports GABA receptor function, and glycine itself is an inhibitory neurotransmitter that independently promotes sleep[A5]. Recommended: Doctor's Best High Absorption Magnesium (glycinate/lysinate chelate), 200mg before bed. You'll absorb more from 200mg glycinate than 400mg oxide, and glycinate is the best-tolerated form with lowest GI side effect rate[A3].
